How to Order a Pizza

For when you can't be bothered going shopping for ingredients and spending time cooking

Ingredients

  • A phone or a PC
  • Internet Connectivity
  • Money (cash or card)
  • A usable address (whether it's your home or not)
  • A deep hunger which only an overpriced pizza can cure

Cooking Instructions

  1. Using your phone or PC, visit the restaurants website via Google
  2. Browse the menu, adding what you like to the basket
  3. Once you have finished, go to the checkout
  4. Ensure you have the funds to cover whatever you have ordered
  5. Enter your address information
  6. Enter your debit/credit card details, otherwise request to pay in cash at the door
  7. Refuse to sign up for any marketing e-mails
  8. Once payment has been accepted, wait for delivery
  9. Enjoy your food!
